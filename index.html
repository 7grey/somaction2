Who We Are

Soma-Action International Fund

SOMA-ACTION has over twenty years (20) of experience in designing and implementing community-based relief, rehabilitation, and development and peace projects. One of the major assets of SOMA-ACTION is its qualified professionals who have experience in the fields of program design and implementation. During its existence, the organization has managed to conduct various needs assessments through participatory methods to assess the priority needs of the local people.

OUR PARTNERS

Advancing multi-stakeholder partnerships for the Sustainable Development Goals

We believe that solving the complex challenges of poverty and social injustice can best be achieved by working with others. SAIF is committed to operational partnerships that are empowering, respectful, and focused on mutual learning and growth. We know that partnership amplifies our impact, and that poverty and social injustice can best be addressed through strategic and impactful alliances with a range of actors from government, civil society, private sector, and beyond.
