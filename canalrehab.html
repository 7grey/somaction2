Canal Rehabilitation For Food Production

Canal rehabilitation plays a crucial role in enhancing food production and ensuring sustainable agriculture.

WHAT WE DO

Canal Rehabilitation For Food Production

In Somali, local farmers construct and manage canal-fed irrigation systems in the mid-hills. These systems carry water from small rivers, streams, and rivulets to cultivable areas. They focus on rehabilitating malfunctioning or non-functioning existing systems in the plains and valley floors. The principles guiding construction/rehabilitation include ensuring a minimum source yield, meeting irrigation demand, limiting canal length, and expanding command areas for small farmholders.
