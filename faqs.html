SOMA-ACTION is a Non-Governmental Organization formed in 1992 and based in Elwaq-District Gedo region and Lower Shebelle Region of Somalia. With its main office in Elwak, it has staff in El Waq and Marka Somalia.


  • WASH Programs include water infrastructures construction and rehabilitation, sanitation in schools and MCH where latrine construction infrastructure is built and promotion of CLTS triggering the community to eliminate open defecation and educate communities on good hygiene practices promotion
  • Food distribution under FFA, FFT, and Resilience program
  • Relief/Drought & locust response food voucher and cash transfer program
  • CASH distribution to families to buy food instead of food distribution
  • Canal rehabilitation for food production
  • Dam construction and rehabilitation of water pans
  • Peace Building programs
  • Policy development framework
