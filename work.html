Soma-Action International Fund

SOMA-ACTION is a Non-Governmental Organization formed in 1992 and based in Elwaq-District Gedo region and Lower Shebelle Region of Somalia. With its main office in Elwak, it has staff in El Waq and Marka Somalia. SOMA-ACTION has over twenty years (20) of experience in designing and implementing communitybased relief, rehabilitation, and development and peace projects. One of the major assets of SOMA-ACTION is its qualified professionals who have experience in the fields of program design and implementation. During its existence, the organization has managed to conduct various needs assessments through participatory methods to assess the priority needs of the local people. SOMA-ACTION has a wealth of experience in partnership building and collaborated with various UN agencies, International NGOs, and members of the community to implement programs and achieve its goals.
